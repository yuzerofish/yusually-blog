Gameplay

Core Loop

Not about pushing harder. About letting go.

Swipe the 4x4 board in any direction and merge matching states. Each merge avoids noisy explosions: pressure cracks, falls into sand, softens into water, opens into breath, and finally turns into light.

Soft feedback

Stones crack gently, sand falls slowly, and the board stays calm instead of interrupting you.

Breath chains

Multiple merges in one swipe become a quiet breath chain. Single moves stay understated.

Gentle endings

A full board is not framed as failure. The game simply suggests making room again.
