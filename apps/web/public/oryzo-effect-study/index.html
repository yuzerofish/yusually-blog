Abstract

We present Oryzo-1, an open-weight 3D model of a cork coaster for rendering, simulation, and gloriously unnecessary research. Oryzo-1 faithfully reproduces key coaster behaviors: table protection, circularity, and passive thermal moderation under everyday beverage conditions.
