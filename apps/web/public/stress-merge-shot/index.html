Gameplay

Core loop

Aim softly. Let matching states meet.

Drag from the bottom of the screen to choose an angle, then release. Same-stage orbs merge into the next pressure state. Different states stay on the board, turning each shot into a small decision about space, timing, and calm.

Bank-shot aiming

Use wall rebounds to reach crowded corners instead of forcing every shot straight upward.

Breath chains

Two or more merges in one launch become a breath combo, giving the board a brief moment of lift.

Gentle endings

A full palm is not failure. The round closes softly and invites another try.
