Zen Stress Shot

Aim softly, clear matching pressure pieces, and let a small zen garden open back up.

A calm bubble-shooter inspired puzzle about releasing suspended stress clusters without timers, loud rewards, or failure language.

How to play: drag from the bottom cup, release the current pressure piece, and connect matching pieces in the honeycomb structure. Clear support points to let whole sections fall away.

Goal: keep the garden open, turn clutter into space, and continue until every pressure piece has been released.
