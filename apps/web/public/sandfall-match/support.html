Support

This page is the support destination for Sandfall Match, a small one-board calm match-3 puzzle.

Gameplay Help

  • Swap adjacent movable tiles to match three or more.
  • Locks cannot move. Clear beside a lock twice to open it.
  • Lights cannot be swapped directly. Clear the path beneath each light until it reaches the sand bed.
  • The game is intentionally designed around one focused board.
