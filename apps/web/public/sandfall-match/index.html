Sandfall Match

A calm match-3 iOS game about clearing pressure stones, opening locks, and letting light fall to the bottom.

A focused one-board calm puzzle for iPhone.

How It Plays

Swap to match

Tap adjacent movable tiles and match three or more to clear pressure from the board.

Open locks

Locks cannot move. Make matches beside them twice to loosen the path.

Guide lights

Clear beneath each light until it falls into the sand bed at the bottom.

Current Scope

Sandfall Match is intentionally scoped as a one-board calm puzzle: a quiet board, a clear goal, and a soft rhythm of pressure stones, locks, sand, water, and light.
